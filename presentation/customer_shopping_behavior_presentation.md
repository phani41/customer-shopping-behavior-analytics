# Customer Shopping Behavior Analytics - Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Problem Statement
Understand customer shopping behavior to improve revenue, retention, and campaign performance.

## Slide 2: Data and Method
- Source: transaction-level retail dataset
- Tools: Python (Pandas), SQL, Power BI
- Workflow: Cleaning -> EDA -> Segmentation -> Dashboard -> Recommendations

## Slide 3: EDA Highlights
- Revenue concentration by category and city
- Payment method trends
- Discount vs order value relationships

## Slide 4: Segmentation
- RFM model to classify Champion, Potential Loyalist, and At Risk cohorts
- Segment-level purchase and value patterns

## Slide 5: Dashboard
- KPI summary cards
- Category, geography, and payment behavior visuals
- Segment monitoring and drilldowns

## Slide 6: Actions
- Retain Champions via loyalty programs
- Nurture Potential Loyalists with tailored campaigns
- Win back At Risk customers with targeted offers
